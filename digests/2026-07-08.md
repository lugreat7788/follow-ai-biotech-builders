## AI 蛋白/基因组设计 · AI Protein & Genome Design

**Institute for Protein Design (David Baker Lab)** — 7 月 2 日于 *Science* 发表 WRAP（Water-soluble RFdiffused Amphipathic Proteins）研究：AI 设计的两亲性蛋白质可将跨膜蛋白包裹并溶于水相，无需去垢剂即可保留天然结构，并首次实现无去垢剂条件下的高分辨率（2.95 Å）膜蛋白结构解析；梅毒外膜蛋白抗原已成功以此方式制备，可直接用于疫苗开发。 / AI-designed amphipathic proteins wrap membrane targets and keep them soluble in water without detergents, enabling structure determination and vaccine antigen production for previously intractable proteins including syphilis outer-membrane proteins.
- 来源：[Baker Lab blog](https://www.bakerlab.org/2026/07/02/a-wrap-for-biologys-greasiest-problem/) · [*Science* DOI:10.1126/science.adr3817](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.adr3817) · [EurekAlert](https://www.eurekalert.org/news-releases/1133699) · [C&EN](https://cen.acs.org/physical-chemistry/protein-folding/ai-designed-proteins-stabilize-unruly-membrane-proteins/104/web/2026/07)

**Mohammed AlQuraishi (Columbia)** — 受邀出席 CS3D Biological Function 2026 会议（摘要截止 7 月 3 日），其 AI 蛋白结构预测工作被 *Molecular Cell* 特别推介；团队近期发布 Genie 3 蛋白设计模型（基于原子级 SE(3)-等变性），并在 *Nature Methods* 发表 ROCKET——在 AlphaFold2 基础上融合冷冻电镜/X 射线数据进行结构精修。 / Invited to CS3D 2026; lab released Genie 3 protein design model and ROCKET (AlphaFold2 augmentation with cryo-EM/X-ray refinement) in *Nature Methods*.
- 来源：[Molecular Cell tweet](https://x.com/MolecularCell/status/2057160656902644020) · [Nature Methods paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-026-03047-4)
