## AI 蛋白质与基因组设计 / AI Protein & Genome Design

**Sergey Ovchinnikov (MIT) et al.** — 4 月 30 日在 *Science* 发表"Toward life with a 19-amino acid alphabet through generative AI design"，利用 AlphaFold2 与生成式 AI 重新设计大肠杆菌核糖体的 382 个异亮氨酸（Ile）残基，产生首个能以 19 种氨基酸正常生长的活细胞 Ec19，开辟人工设计"最小遗传密码"方向。Ovchinnikov 为通讯作者之一。
The team used generative AI and AlphaFold2 to redesign all 382 isoleucine residues in E. coli ribosomes, generating the living strain Ec19—the first organism to function with just 19 canonical amino acids—opening a new frontier for synthetic biology and minimal genetic codes.
→ [Science paper](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.aeb5171) | [Science News](https://www.science.org/content/article/ai-helps-create-bacterium-s-partially-missing-universal-amino-acid) | [Scientific American](https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/scientists-use-ai-to-test-whether-life-can-run-on-only-19-amino-acids/)
