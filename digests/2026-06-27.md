## AI 蛋白质与分子设计 / AI Protein & Molecular Design

**Chai Discovery (Joshua Meier, CEO)** — 6 月 2 日与辉瑞签署授权协议，辉瑞将获得最新 Chai-3 模型访问权及基于辉瑞私有数据微调的定制模型；Chai-3 在从头抗体设计的成功率相较 Chai-2 翻倍，可处理难成药靶点（GPCRs 等）及多特异性分子。/ Chai Discovery licensed its Chai-3 AI platform to Pfizer on June 2, granting Pfizer both access to the frontier model and a custom fine-tuned version on Pfizer's proprietary data; Chai-3 doubles antibody design success rates vs. the previous generation.
- [BusinessWire — June 2, 2026](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260602498831/en/Chai-Discovery-Announces-License-Agreement-with-Pfizer-to-Accelerate-Drug-Discovery-with-AI)
- [Healthcare MEA — June 9 analysis](https://www.healthcaremea.com/2026/06/09/pfizer-expands-ai-drug-discovery-strategy-through-licensing-agreement-with-chai-discovery/)

**Institute for Protein Design / David Baker (UW)** — 华盛顿研究基金会（WRF）拨款 $700 万，支持 IPD 新四年酶设计项目，资金从 2026 年中开始拨付；项目目标是强化"设计-构建-测试-学习"管线并将发现成果转化为商业工具、培育新创公司。/ Washington Research Foundation awarded $7M to David Baker's UW Institute for Protein Design for a four-year AI-enabled enzyme design initiative starting mid-2026, focused on strengthening the design-build-test-learn pipeline and launching startups.
- [GeekWire](https://www.geekwire.com/2026/from-computer-to-lab-to-market-nobel-winner-david-baker-lands-7m-for-new-protein-program/)
- [WRF press release](https://www.wrfseattle.org/news/washington-research-foundation-awards-7-million-to-uw-institute-for-protein-design-for-ambitious-initiative-to-advance-ai-enabled-enzyme-design/)

**Simon Kohl / Latent Labs** — 前 DeepMind AlphaFold2 核心成员，在 CVPR 2026（丹佛，6 月 17–21 日）发表大会主旨演讲《可编程生物学：分子设计的生成 AI》；同期发布 Latent-Y，支持从文本提示到实验室就绪序列的端到端全自主蛋白质设计流程。/ Ex-DeepMind AlphaFold2 researcher Simon Kohl gave a CVPR 2026 plenary keynote on "Programmable Biology: Generative AI for Molecular Design"; Latent Labs also announced Latent-Y, an end-to-end autonomous protein design campaign from text prompt to lab-ready sequences.
