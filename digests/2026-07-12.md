## Arc Institute — AI 生物学全栈 / Arc Institute's Full AI Biology Stack

**Patrick Hsu et al. (Arc Institute) — Evo 2 登 *Nature*** — 2026 年 3 月正式发表于 *Nature*（"Genome modelling and design across all domains of life with Evo 2"），模型规模 400 亿参数、100 万核苷酸上下文窗口，训练于 12.8 万余物种的 9.3 万亿核苷酸（跨细菌、古菌、真核生物）；零样本即可预测 BRCA1 等临床变体的功能影响，也是迄今唯一同时覆盖编码与非编码突变的开源基因组基础模型。Arc 于近期同步发布"Evo 2 一周年"回顾，展示在合成生物学设计任务中的实验验证进展。/ Evo 2 officially published in *Nature* (March 2026): 40B parameters, 1M-nucleotide context, trained on 9.3T nucleotides from 128,000+ species across all domains of life. Zero-shot prediction of functional impacts of clinically significant variants (BRCA1 and others), coding and noncoding alike. Open-sourced; Arc also released an "Evo 2: One Year Later" retrospective with experimental validation highlights.

**Brian Hie 实验室 (Arc Institute) — Proto 编排框架** — 6 月 23 日在 bioRxiv 发布预印本，可视为 Evo 2 之上的"编排层"：以 Python 式高层语法将蛋白质设计、RNA 工程、小分子对接等孤立 AI 模型组合为端到端设计活动（Campaign），让实验生物学家无需深厚计算背景也能运行复杂的多模态设计工作流。Arc 由此完成"感知（Evo 2）→ 编排（Proto）→ 实验验证"的 AI 生物学闭环。/ Proto (bioRxiv, June 23) sits above Evo 2 as an orchestration layer—a high-level programming language that composes isolated protein-design, RNA-engineering, and ligand-docking AI models into multi-objective end-to-end design campaigns, lowering the barrier for experimental biologists. Together with Evo 2, Arc now offers a "perceive → orchestrate → validate" AI biology loop.
