# AI × 生物医药 Builder 动态摘要 | 2026-07-20

## 概览 / Overview

本周最亮眼的两件事同日发生（7月14日）：**Chai Discovery 完成 4 亿美元 C 轮**融资（估值 38 亿美元），**Demis Hassabis** 同步发布个人宣言，呼吁美国主导建立 AI 前沿模型监管机构。更早些，**Insilico Medicine** 与武田达成 6 亿美元 AI 药物发现合作（7月2日），**UW 蛋白设计研究所** 公布 700 万美元新资助并发布膜蛋白新技术。资本与技术双轮驱动，AI×生物医药本周明显提速。

The week's standout double on July 14: **Chai Discovery** closed a **$400M Series C** (valued at $3.8B), and **Demis Hassabis** simultaneously published a manifesto calling for a US-led frontier-AI standards body. Earlier, **Insilico Medicine** sealed a **$600M Takeda deal** (July 2), and the **UW Institute for Protein Design** won $7M in fresh funding while debuting a membrane-protein technique. Capital and technical progress both accelerated this week.

## 一、AI 监管 / AI Regulation & Policy

**Demis Hassabis（Google DeepMind CEO）** — 7月14日发布个人倡议文章《前沿 AI 框架》，呼吁美国主导建立类似 FINRA 的独立 AI 标准机构：前沿模型上线前须提交最多30天的安全审查，由图灵奖得主等独立专家主导；本周赴华盛顿游说政策制定者，目标年底前机构落地。/ Published "A Framework for Frontier AI" on July 14, calling for a US-led independent AI standards body modeled on FINRA — frontier models reviewed up to 30 days pre-release by a board led by Turing Award winners; plans Washington lobbying meetings this week, targeting a body operational before year-end.

## 二、AI 蛋白 & 分子设计 / AI Protein & Molecular Design

**UW Institute for Protein Design（David Baker）** — 7月2日：获华盛顿研究基金会 700 万美元四年期资助，专项支持 AI 驱动酶设计"设计-构建-测试-学习"闭环；同日 Baker Lab 发布利用计算设计"WRAPs"无需洗涤剂即可溶解膜蛋白的新方法，为膜蛋白药物靶点研究开辟新路径。/ On July 2: Won $7M WRF grant for AI enzyme design build-test-learn pipeline; Baker Lab also published computationally designed "WRAPs" that solubilize membrane proteins without detergents.

**Alexander Rives（Chan Zuckerberg Biohub）** — 6月发布 ESMFold2：60 亿参数蛋白语言模型，仅靠序列数据（无任何人工标注生物知识）即自主学会蛋白质折叠规律，并能从头设计治疗级癌症抗体结合物；配套 5 亿美元"虚拟生物学计划"同步启动，含冷冻电镜、空间生物等实验平台建设。/ Released ESMFold2 in June: a 6B-parameter protein language model that learned protein folding purely from sequences, now designing cancer antibody binders; paired with a $500M Virtual Biology Initiative funding experimental platforms.

**Profluent / Ali Madani** — 与礼来签署 22.5 亿美元合作，使用 AI 设计重组酶实现大片段基因整合（超越点编辑 CRISPR），目标改写遗传医学赛道；同期在 *Nature Biotechnology* 发表 Protein2PAM 模型（AI 快速设计新 PAM 特异性 CRISPR 编辑器）并开源。/ Signed $2.25B deal with Eli Lilly to develop AI-designed recombinases for whole-gene insertion beyond CRISPR; published Protein2PAM in *Nature Biotechnology* for rapid CRISPR editor design, released open-source.

**Stef van Grieken（Cradle）** — 7月中旬接受专访，明确 AI agent 在生命科学中的落地边界：代码生成可全自动，但湿实验反馈依然不可替代；指出行业对"AI 开发药物"的误解——生物实验不等于写代码。/ Mid-July interview clarifying AI agent limits in life sciences: code generation is fully automatable, but wet-lab feedback loops remain irreplaceable; corrects industry misunderstanding that AI bio-drug development works like AI code generation.

## 四、医学 AI / Medical AI Research

**Pranav Rajpurkar（Harvard）** — 6月18日在 *NEJM AI* 发表 ReXGroundingCT：首个将自由文本胸部CT报告中的发现手动关联到3D分割掩码的公开基准数据集，为"接地气"影像报告生成 AI 奠定评测基础；数据集已开放在 HuggingFace。/ Published ReXGroundingCT in *NEJM AI* on June 18: first manually curated benchmark linking free-text chest CT findings to 3D segmentation masks; dataset public on HuggingFace.
