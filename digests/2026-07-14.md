## 🩺 AI 临床医学 | Clinical AI

**Eric Topol / Ground Truths** — 6 月中旬发表长文《Agentic AI Comes to Medicine》，系统梳理两篇 Nature 论文：德国 MIRA（急诊全流程 AI Agent，比肩四位主治医）和 Google MIRA/AMIE（门诊纵向管理 AI Agent），是 AI 从"辅助"跨向"自主"医疗决策的里程碑综述。 / Published "Agentic AI Comes to Medicine" (mid-June, Ground Truths/Substack): reviews two landmark Nature papers showing AI agents (MIRA and AMIE) autonomously managing emergency and outpatient care, marking a shift from AI assistance to AI-driven clinical decision-making.

**Pranav Rajpurkar (Harvard)** — 在 Nature Medicine（March 2026）发表临床 AI 评估新框架：通过模拟数字医院环境测试 AI 在动态临床决策中的级联影响，填补了现有基准无法反映真实部署场景的空白。 / Published clinical AI evaluation framework in Nature Medicine (March 2026): simulates digital hospital environments to evaluate AI under real-world clinical decision cascades—a benchmark advance beyond static test sets.
