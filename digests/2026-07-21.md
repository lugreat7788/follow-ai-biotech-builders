## AI 监管 / AI Policy & Governance

**Demis Hassabis（DeepMind）** — 7 月 14 日发文《前沿 AI 与新纪元框架》，提议建立 FINRA 式行业自律监管机构，对前沿模型上市前进行安全评估，并可在必要时协调全行业减速；呼吁年底前正式运作，获 OpenAI、微软、Elon Musk 等罕见背书。  
Published "A Framework for Frontier AI and the Dawning of a New Age" on July 14, proposing a FINRA-modeled US-led standards body to screen frontier models pre-release, with power to coordinate industry-wide slowdowns; rare cross-industry praise from Altman, Nadella, and Musk.

## AI 蛋白/药物设计 / AI Protein & Drug Design

**Profluent（Ali Madani）** — 7 月宣布与农业科技巨头 Corteva Agriscience 合作，将 ProGen3 和 OpenCRISPR-1 平台应用于高产、抗旱、抗病虫农作物蛋白设计；此前已签 Eli Lilly $2.25B 重组酶合作协议（4 月）及 $106M B 轮融资。  
July: Partnership with Corteva Agriscience to engineer crop proteins using ProGen3 and OpenCRISPR-1 AI platforms — targeting yield, drought, and pest resistance. Follows a $2.25B Eli Lilly deal (April) and $106M Series B.  
→ [SynBioBeta](https://www.synbiobeta.com/read/profluent-and-corteva-partner-to-apply-ai-designed-proteins-in-agriculture) · [BioSpace](https://www.biospace.com/press-releases/profluent-announces-strategic-partnership-with-lilly-to-develop-ai-designed-recombinases-for-genetic-medicine)

**Isomorphic Labs（Demis Hassabis）** — 在 Davos 确认首批 AI 设计药物将于 2026 年底进入临床；IsoDDE（药物设计引擎）已在 AlphaFold 3 基础上新增结合亲和力预测、隐蔽口袋识别等功能，标志从结构预测迈向真正药物设计。  
Confirmed at Davos: first AI-designed drug candidates entering clinical trials by end of 2026. IsoDDE engine extends AlphaFold 3 with binding-affinity estimation, cryptic-pocket identification, and antibody-antigen modeling.  
→ [IntuitionLabs](https://intuitionlabs.ai/articles/isomorphic-labs-alphafold-ai-drug-discovery-trials)

**Alexander Rives（Arc/Biohub）& ESM** — Biohub 发布 ESMFold2 + ESMC + ESM Atlas「蛋白质生物学世界模型」套件，在抗体设计和微型蛋白 binder 上达到 SOTA 表现。  
Biohub released ESMFold2, ESMC, and ESM Atlas as a unified "world model of protein biology," achieving state-of-the-art performance on antibody and miniprotein binder design.  
→ [Biohub](https://biohub.org/news/world-model-of-protein-biology/)

**UW Institute for Protein Design** — 3 月获 Washington Research Foundation 700 万美元资助，启动 AI 赋能酶设计新项目，由诺贝尔奖得主 David Baker 主导，目标横跨医疗、能源与材料。  
Received $7M grant from Washington Research Foundation (March) to launch a Nobel-winner David Baker-led AI-enabled enzyme design initiative spanning medicine, energy, and materials.
