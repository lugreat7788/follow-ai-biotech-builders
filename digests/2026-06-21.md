## AI 蛋白/药物设计 / AI Protein & Drug Design

**Eric Topol** — 在 Ground Truths 发表《Agentic AI Comes to Medicine》，解读《Nature》上两项端到端医疗智能体研究：德国团队的 MIRA 和 Google 的 AMIE。在 500 个模拟急诊病例中，MIRA 可自主问诊、开化验/影像/用药/手术医嘱并分诊住院，诊断准确率达 87.8%，高于执业医师的 78.1%；Topol 强调这仍是仿真环境下的结果，距离真实临床应用还需随机对照试验验证。/ Published "Agentic AI Comes to Medicine" on Ground Truths, breaking down two Nature studies on end-to-end medical AI agents: Germany's MIRA and Google's AMIE. Across 500 simulated emergency-department cases, MIRA autonomously took histories, ordered labs/imaging/medications/procedures, and triaged admissions, hitting 87.8% diagnostic accuracy versus 78.1% for board-certified physicians — though Topol cautions this is still a simulation, and real-world adoption would need randomized trials.
来源 / Source: https://erictopol.substack.com/p/agentic-ai-comes-to-medicine （约 2026-06-20）

**Jure Leskovec** — AACR 官方博客本周专题报道其团队开发的通用生物医学 AI 智能体 Biomni：集成 150 个生物医学工具、105 个软件包与 59 个数据库，可自主完成文献综述、假设生成、实验方案设计、生物信息学分析等任务，现已开源并衍生出由 a16z 参投的商业化项目。/ AACR's official blog ran a feature this week on Biomni, the general-purpose biomedical AI agent built by Leskovec's team — integrating 150 biomedical tools, 105 software packages, and 59 databases to autonomously handle literature review, hypothesis generation, protocol design, and bioinformatics analysis. It's open source and has since spun out into an a16z-backed commercial venture.
来源 / Source: https://www.aacr.org/blog/2026/06/18/eye-on-ai-agentic-ai-brings-bots-to-the-bench/ （2026-06-18）
