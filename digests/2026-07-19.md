## AI 医疗 · AI in Medicine

**Eric Topol（Ground Truths）** — 发布《Agentic AI Comes to Medicine》，解析两篇 Nature 论文（MIRA 和 Google AMIE），展示自主 AI Agent 已能完成急诊病例端到端诊疗方案，强调需严格验证儿童/青少年适用性。/ Published "Agentic AI Comes to Medicine," reviewing two Nature papers (MIRA from Kather et al. and Google's AMIE) showing AI agents can manage full ED case workups end-to-end; emphasizes rigorous validation requirements for pediatric populations.
- 来源: [Ground Truths / Substack](https://erictopol.substack.com/p/agentic-ai-comes-to-medicine)

**Pranav Rajpurkar（Harvard）** — 在 Nature Medicine 发表 Clinical Environment Simulator（CES），首个在动态数字医院环境中评估临床 LLM 的框架——每个决策都会改变后续病人状态。/ Published the Clinical Environment Simulator (CES) in Nature Medicine — the first framework evaluating clinical LLMs in a dynamic digital hospital where every AI decision alters future patient state.
- 来源: [Nature Medicine](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04252-6)
