## AI 制药公司动态 / AI Pharma Company News

**Recursion Pharmaceuticals (RXRX)** — 在 6 月 17 日的年度股东大会上，公司选举 Najat Khan（现任 CEO，曾任强生首席数据科学官）与 Franziska Michor（哈佛大学/Dana-Farber 癌症研究所计算生物学教授）为新晋二级董事；联合创始人 Chris Gibson 任期届满后未寻求连任，正式退出董事会（此前已于今年初卸任 CEO 转任董事长），此后仅以战略顾问身份参与；公司现金储备约 6.545 亿美元，预计可支撑运营至 2028 年初。/ At its June 17 annual shareholder meeting, the company elected Najat Khan (current CEO, former J&J Chief Data Science Officer) and Franziska Michor (computational biology professor at Harvard/Dana-Farber) as new Class II directors; co-founder Chris Gibson completed his board term and did not seek re-election, formally exiting the board (having already stepped down as CEO to chairman earlier this year) and continuing only as a strategic advisor going forward; the company holds roughly $654.5M in cash, with runway into early 2028.
