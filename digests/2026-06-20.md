**Simon Kohl (Latent Labs)** — 在 CVPR 2026 做大会主题演讲《Programmable Biology: Generative AI for Molecular Design》，介绍 Latent-X1 大环肽/迷你结合蛋白生成模型；并出席伦敦科技周（6 月 8–12 日）。/ Delivered a CVPR 2026 plenary keynote, "Programmable Biology: Generative AI for Molecular Design," covering Latent Labs' generative models (incl. 来源 / Source: https://www.eurekalert.org/news-releases/1129114 ｜ https://newsroom.uw.edu/news-releases/ai-helps-create-miniprotein-switches-for-drug-targets/ （2026-05-21，略早于本周但影响较大）

**Alexander Rives / Sergey Ovchinnikov** — Rives 领导的 Chan Zuckerberg Biohub 团队发布开源 ESMFold2 + ESM-C + ESM Atlas，生成超10亿个蛋白结构预测，并针对 EGFR、PD-L1、CTLA-4 等5个癌症/免疫靶点设计出经实验验证的高亲和力结合蛋白；MIT 的 Sergey Ovchinnikov 公开评论称其完全开源、可商用，将与 AlphaFold 数据库形成互补。/ Rives's team at Chan Zuckerberg Biohub released open-source ESMFold2 + ESM-C + ESM Atlas, generating 1B+ predicted protein structures and producing lab-validated binders against 5 cancer/immunology targets (EGFR, PD-L1, CTLA-4, etc.); MIT's Sergey Ovchinnikov publicly noted it's fully open and commercially usable, complementing the AlphaFold database.
