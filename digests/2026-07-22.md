## 二、AI 蛋白质与药物设计 / AI Protein & Drug Design

**Institute for Protein Design / Baker Lab（@UWproteindesign）** — 7 月 2 日《Science》发表 WRAP：AI 设计的两亲性蛋白可无需去垢剂地包裹并溶解膜蛋白，分辨率 2.95 Å；解锁疫苗、诊断和药物靶向膜蛋白的实验通路。/ Published in *Science* (July 2): AI-designed WRAP protein sheaths solubilize membrane proteins without detergents at 2.95 Å resolution, unlocking vaccine, diagnostic, and drug R&D against this notoriously difficult protein class.
- 来源：[Baker Lab](https://www.bakerlab.org/2026/07/02/a-wrap-for-biologys-greasiest-problem/) · [Phys.org](https://phys.org/news/2026-07-biology-greasiest-problem.html) · [Chemistry World](https://www.chemistryworld.com/news/protein-wrappers-designed-by-ai-solve-membrane-proteins-solubility-problem/4023797.article)

**Alexander Rives / Chan Zuckerberg Biohub** — 5 月 27 日发布蛋白质「世界模型」：ESMFold2 在抗体-抗原复合物预测上优于 AlphaFold 3；ESM Atlas 收录 68 亿蛋白质、11 亿预测结构；全部开源免费。/ Released "world model of protein biology" (May 27): ESMFold2 outperforms AlphaFold 3 on antibody-antigen complexes; ESM Atlas indexes 6.8B proteins and 1.1B structures; fully open-source.
- 来源：[Biohub](https://biohub.org/news/world-model-of-protein-biology/) · [GEN](https://www.genengnews.com/topics/artificial-intelligence/biohub-releases-protein-biology-world-model-to-address-disease/) · [pharmaphorum](https://pharmaphorum.com/news/zuckerberg-chans-biohub-launches-protein-world-model)

**Simon Kohl / Latent Labs（@saakohl）** — 3 月发布的 Latent-Y 全自主抗体设计智能体持续引发关注：9 个靶点中 6 个成功（67%），单数字纳摩尔亲和力，无人工筛选干预，端到端实验室验证；较专家人工估计快 56 倍。/ Latent-Y autonomous antibody design agent (March 2026) continues to circulate: 67% target success across 9 targets at single-digit nM affinity, end-to-end lab-validated, 56× faster than expert estimates, no human filtering required.
- 来源：[VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/business/latent-y-the-autonomous-ai-agent-drug-design-at-scale) · [SynBioBeta](https://www.synbiobeta.com/read/inside-latent-labs-autonomous-drug-design-engine)

**Arc Institute（@arcinstitute）** — 3 月 Evo 2 正式发表于 *Nature*：400 亿参数 DNA 基础模型，训练于 12.8 万物种、9.3 万亿核苷酸 token，可识别致病突变并从头设计新基因组；与 NVIDIA BioNeMo 集成，代码开源。/ Evo 2 published in *Nature* (March 2026): 40B-parameter DNA foundation model trained on 9.3T nucleotide tokens from 128K species across all domains of life; identifies disease-causing mutations and designs novel genomes; integrated with NVIDIA BioNeMo.
- 来源：[Arc Institute](https://arcinstitute.org/news/evo2) · [Phys.org](https://phys.org/news/2026-03-evo-ai-genetic-code-domains.html) · [GEN](https://www.genengnews.com/gen-edge/arc-institutes-ai-model-designs-the-genetic-code-across-all-domains-of-life/)
