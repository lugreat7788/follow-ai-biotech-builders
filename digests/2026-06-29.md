## 一、AI 蛋白质与分子设计 / AI Protein & Molecular Design

**Institute for Protein Design (UW)** — 6/23 宣布与 NVIDIA 合作，将 BioNeMo Agent Toolkit（含 cuEquivariance、TensorRT）集成至 RF3 流水线，256 残基蛋白推断时间从 5.8 秒降至 2.5 秒（缩短 57%），大幅提升实验室可测试候选蛋白的通量。/ On Jun 23, IPD & NVIDIA announced integration of the BioNeMo Agent Toolkit into the RF3 pipeline, cutting inference time from 5.8 s to 2.5 s on a 256-residue protein — enabling the team to filter far more design candidates before wet-lab testing.
- 来源：[IPD News](https://www.ipd.uw.edu/2026/06/custom-computing-for-the-next-generation-of-protein-design/) | [NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-launches-bionemo-agent-toolkit-giving-ai-agents-the-tools-to-accelerate-scientific-discovery)

**Arc Institute (Brian Hie Lab)** — 6/23 在 bioRxiv 发布 Proto：一种面向生成生物学的高层编程语言，可跨 DNA、RNA、蛋白质、配体等模态组合不同 AI 工具，执行多目标序列设计任务。/ On Jun 23, the Hie Lab posted Proto on bioRxiv — a high-level programming language for generative biology that composes AI models across DNA, RNA, protein, and ligand modalities to satisfy multi-objective design constraints.
- 来源：[bioRxiv preprint](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.06.22.733870v1) | [Arc Institute](https://arcinstitute.org/news/proto)

**Alexander Rives / BioHub** — 约 6/9 正式开源 ESMFold2：蛋白质生物学"世界模型"，已预测 11 亿条蛋白质结构，并在实验室中成功验证了针对癌症/免疫学 5 个靶点的高亲和力蛋白质结合剂。/ Around Jun 9, BioHub (Alex Rives, Head of Science) publicly released ESMFold2 — an open-source protein biology world model covering 1.1 billion predicted structures, with lab-validated high-affinity binders against five cancer/immunology disease targets.
- 来源：[BioHub News](https://biohub.org/news/world-model-of-protein-biology/) | [Lab Manager](https://www.labmanager.com/how-a-new-protein-folding-ai-generates-over-one-billion-structures-for-research-35487) | [Genetic Engineering News](https://www.genengnews.com/topics/artificial-intelligence/biohub-releases-protein-biology-world-model-to-address-disease/)

**Simon Kohl / Latent Labs** — 6/5 在 CVPR 2026（丹佛）发表主题演讲「Programmable Biology: Generative AI for Molecular Design」，展示 Latent-X1（大环肽 & 迷你蛋白结合剂）、Latent-X2（抗体——首款经人血捐赠者实验验证无免疫原性的 AI 设计抗体）以及 Latent-Y（文本驱动的结构设计 AI 代理）。/ On Jun 5, Simon Kohl (ex-AlphaFold2 co-creator, CEO of Latent Labs) gave a CVPR 2026 plenary on generative molecular design, unveiling Latent-X1 (macrocyclic peptides & mini-binders), Latent-X2 (antibodies with no immunogenic response in human blood donor assays — a first for AI-designed antibodies), and Latent-Y (an AI agent for text-prompt-driven structure-based design campaigns).
- 来源：[CVPR 2026 Talk](https://cvpr.thecvf.com/virtual/2026/invited-talk/40397) | [Latent Labs Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/latent-labs-launches-based-ai-060000340.html) | [Endpoints News](https://endpoints.news/ex-deepmind-scientist-launches-ai-biotech-latent-labs-with-50m/)
