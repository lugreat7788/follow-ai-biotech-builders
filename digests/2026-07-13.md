# AI × 生物医药 Builder 日报 · 2026-07-13

## 概览 / Overview

本周最值得关注的三件事：Insilico Medicine 宣布将 AI 发现的药物 rentosertib 推进至 Phase III 临床，成为迄今最接近获批的"完全 AI 发现"分子；UW 蛋白质设计研究所在 *Science* 发表 WRAP 突破，用 AI 设计蛋白质解决困扰生物学家数十年的膜蛋白溶解难题；Eric Topol 与 Tony Wyss-Coray 在 *Nature Medicine* 联合发表生物衰老时钟综述，将老龄化研究的视角从"年龄"切换至"器官/细胞时钟"。

**The three highlights this week:** Insilico Medicine entered Phase III for rentosertib — the most advanced fully AI-discovered drug to date; UW IPD published a WRAP breakthrough in *Science* that makes membrane proteins water-soluble without detergents; and Eric Topol co-authored a landmark *Nature Medicine* review shifting aging research from calendar age to biological clocks.

## AI 蛋白质 / 基因组设计 · AI Protein & Genome Design

**Institute for Protein Design (David Baker lab, @UWproteindesign)** — 7 月 2 日在 *Science* 发表 WRAP 技术：用计算设计的两亲性蛋白将膜蛋白包裹后直接从水溶液中纯化，跳过去垢剂步骤，首个应用靶点为梅毒疫苗抗原。同期宣布获得 Washington Research Foundation 700 万美元四年期资助，用于 AI 设计酶的商业化转化。
*Published WRAP technology in Science (July 2): computationally designed amphipathic proteins solubilize membrane proteins without detergents, with immediate application to a stalled syphilis vaccine antigen. Also announced a $7M WRF grant for commercializing AI-designed enzymes.*
- 来源：[Baker Lab](https://www.bakerlab.org/2026/07/02/a-wrap-for-biologys-greasiest-problem/) · [UW Medicine](https://newsroom.uw.edu/news-releases/a-wrap-for-biologys-greasiest-problem) · [WRF Grant](https://www.wrfseattle.org/news/washington-research-foundation-awards-7-million-to-uw-institute-for-protein-design-for-ambitious-initiative-to-advance-ai-enabled-enzyme-design/)

**Alexander Rives (@alexrives, CZ Biohub)** — 5 月底/6 月发布 ESMFold2：60 亿参数蛋白质"世界模型"，开源，ESM Atlas 收录超 10 亿条蛋白质结构预测，在抗体基准上超越 AlphaFold3。
*Released ESMFold2 (late May/June): a 6B-parameter open-source protein world model with 1B+ structures in the ESM Atlas; outperforms AlphaFold3 on antibody benchmarks.*
- 来源：[CZ Biohub](https://biohub.org/news/world-model-of-protein-biology/) · [GEN Engineering News](https://www.genengnews.com/topics/artificial-intelligence/biohub-releases-protein-biology-world-model-to-address-disease/) · [Scientific American](https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/new-protein-folding-ai-vastly-expands-on-alphafolds-efforts/)

**Chai Discovery / Joshua Meier (@joshim5)** — Chai-3 模型（据称抗体设计成功率较 Chai-2 翻倍）于 6 月随礼来合作悄然部署；6 月 4 日与辉瑞签订许可协议，后者获 Chai-3 早期访问权。据报道正以 34 亿美元估值融资 4 亿美元。
*Chai-3 deployed in June (doubles success rate vs. Chai-2); Pfizer license agreement signed June 4 for early access to Chai-3. Reports put a new $400M raise at a $3.4B valuation in progress.*
- 来源：[Pfizer deal (BusinessWire)](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260602498831/en/Chai-Discovery-Announces-License-Agreement-with-Pfizer-to-Accelerate-Drug-Discovery-with-AI) · [Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/amyfeldman/2026/06/04/why-pfizer-and-eli-lilly-are-betting-on-this-13-billion-ai-drug-discovery-startup/)

**Arc Institute (@arcinstitute)** — Evo 2 基因组基础模型（400 亿参数，覆盖全生命树 10 万+ 物种）于 3 月正式发表于 *Nature*；近期启动 AIxBio 本科生 Fellows 项目，从 300+ 申请中选出 5 支团队共 13 名学生开展探索性研究。
*Evo 2 (40B-parameter, all-of-life genomic model) published in Nature March 2026; the new AIxBio Fellows cohort (13 students, 5 teams) was just announced, with several projects building on Evo 2.*

**Profluent / Ali Madani (@thisismadani)** — 4 月 28 日宣布与礼来签订最高 22.5 亿美元合作，用 AI 设计位点特异性重组酶实现精准基因组编辑；并在 *Nature Biotechnology* 发表 Protein2PAM——一种快速设计新 PAM 识别域 CRISPR 编辑器的 AI 模型。
*Announced a $2.25B collaboration with Eli Lilly (April 28) to AI-design site-specific recombinases; also published Protein2PAM in Nature Biotechnology for rapid CRISPR editor design.*
- 来源：[BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260428698315/en/Profluent-Announces-Strategic-Partnership-with-Lilly-to-Develop-AI-Designed-Recombinases-for-Genetic-Medicine) · [BioSpace](https://www.biospace.com/deals/lilly-ai-biotech-profluent-ink-2-25b-pact-in-search-of-genetic-medicine-holy-grail)
