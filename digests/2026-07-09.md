## AI 蛋白质与基因组设计 / AI Protein & Genome Design

**Institute for Protein Design (@UWproteindesign)** — 7 月 2 日在 *Science* 发表 WRAPs（水溶性 RFdiffusion 设计两亲蛋白），首次实现将膜蛋白从细胞膜中完整提取、无需去垢剂，解锁疫苗、诊断与药物发现新应用。/ Published WRAPs in *Science* (Jul 2): AI-designed amphipathic proteins that solubilize membrane proteins without detergents, enabling new applications in vaccines, diagnostics, and drug discovery.
→ [Baker Lab](https://www.bakerlab.org/2026/07/02/a-wrap-for-biologys-greasiest-problem/) · [UW Medicine Newsroom](https://newsroom.uw.edu/news-releases/a-wrap-for-biologys-greasiest-problem) · [C&EN 报道](https://cen.acs.org/physical-chemistry/protein-folding/ai-designed-proteins-stabilize-unruly-membrane-proteins/104/web/2026/07)

**Institute for Protein Design (@UWproteindesign)** — 华盛顿研究基金会同期向大卫·贝克实验室投入 700 万美元（四年期），专项推进 AI 酶设计从计算机到实验室再到市场的完整转化。/ Washington Research Foundation awarded $7M over four years to Baker's lab to accelerate AI-enabled enzyme design from bench to market.
→ [GeekWire](https://www.geekwire.com/2026/from-computer-to-lab-to-market-nobel-winner-david-baker-lands-7m-for-new-protein-program/)

**Chan Zuckerberg Biohub / Alexander Rives (@alexrives)** — 六月发布 ESMFold2 + ESM Atlas（覆盖 10 亿+ 蛋白质结构），作为 5 亿美元「虚拟生物学计划」的核心基础设施，将蛋白质生物学推向「世界模型」阶段。/ Biohub released ESMFold2 and ESM Atlas (1B+ predicted structures) as core infrastructure of the $500M Virtual Biology Initiative (Jun 2026), positioning protein biology as an engineering discipline.
→ [Biohub 新闻](https://biohub.org/news/world-model-of-protein-biology/) · [Scientific American](https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/new-protein-folding-ai-vastly-expands-on-alphafolds-efforts/)

**Mohammed AlQuraishi (@MoAlQuraishi)** — 受邀作为主题演讲人出席 CS3D Biological Function 2026 大会（摘要截止 7 月 3 日），围绕 AI 蛋白质结构预测与计算生物学前沿发表演讲。/ Invited keynote speaker at CS3D Biological Function 2026 (abstract deadline Jul 3), presenting on AI-driven protein structure prediction and computational biology.
