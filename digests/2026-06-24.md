## 🔬 AI 蛋白质 / 基因组设计 | AI Protein & Genome Design

**John Jumper（DeepMind → Anthropic）** — AlphaFold2 共同创造者、2024 年诺贝尔化学奖得主 John Jumper 宣布在 DeepMind 近 9 年后加入 Anthropic，为后者带去顶级科学公信力；Anthropic 此前已开设湿实验室并与 Allen Institute、HHMI 建立生物 AI 合作。 / Co-creator of AlphaFold2 and 2024 Nobel Chemistry co-laureate John Jumper left Google DeepMind after ~9 years to join Anthropic, lending Nobel-grade scientific credibility to Anthropic's growing AI-for-biology push.
- 来源: [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/20/nobel-laureate-john-jumper-is-leaving-deepmind-for-rival-anthropic/) | [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/19/john-jumper-to-leave-google-deepmind-for-anthropic.html)

**Demis Hassabis（Google DeepMind）** — Jumper 与 Noam Shazeer 相继离职后，Hassabis 在戛纳狮子节（6 月 23 日）接受 Semafor 专访，强调 DeepMind 拥有业界"最广最深的研究人才库"；同周他还出席 G7 峰会（6 月 17 日），与 Dario Amodei、Sam Altman 共同呼吁建立美国主导的 AI 联盟。 / After Jumper and Shazeer's exits rattled markets (Google shares fell 7%), Hassabis told Semafor at Cannes Lions (June 23) that DeepMind has "by far the biggest and broadest research bench." He also joined G7 leaders on June 17 to call for a U.S.-led AI coalition.
- 来源: [Semafor](https://www.semafor.com/article/06/23/2026/deepmind-chief-demis-hassabis-says-googles-still-winning-ai-talent) | [Fortune / departures](https://fortune.com/2026/06/23/google-deepmind-ai-researcher-departures-raise-doubts-about-ability-to-win-the-ai-race-shazeer-jumper-eye-on-ai/) | [CNBC / G7](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/anthropic-amodei-google-hassabis-us-ai-coalition-g7.html)

**Arc Institute（Evo 2）** — Arc Institute 的 Evo 2 基因组基础模型（400 亿参数，9 万亿核苷酸训练，覆盖地球全域生命）正式发表于 *Nature*，能精准识别致病突变、从头设计简单菌基因组长度的序列；已完全开源并集成 NVIDIA BioNeMo。 / Arc Institute's Evo 2 — 40B-parameter DNA foundation model trained on 9 trillion nucleotides spanning all domains of life — published in *Nature*; accurately predicts disease-causing mutations and designs genomes from scratch; fully open-sourced and available via NVIDIA BioNeMo.
- 来源: [Arc Institute](https://arcinstitute.org/news/evo2) | [Nature](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-026-10176-5) | [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/evo-2-biomolecular-ai/)

**UW Institute for Protein Design / David Baker** — Washington Research Foundation 向 UW 蛋白质设计研究所拨款 700 万美元，支持 AI 酶设计新项目，诺贝尔奖得主 David Baker 领衔，为期四年自 2026 年中启动，配套还有 Sanford Weill 等捐赠支持。 / Washington Research Foundation awarded $7M to UW Institute for Protein Design to advance AI-enabled enzyme design, led by Nobel laureate David Baker; four-year program starting mid-2026, with additional philanthropic support.
- 来源: [WRF](https://www.wrfseattle.org/news/washington-research-foundation-awards-7-million-to-uw-institute-for-protein-design-for-ambitious-initiative-to-advance-ai-enabled-enzyme-design/) | [GeekWire](https://www.geekwire.com/2026/from-computer-to-lab-to-market-nobel-winner-david-baker-lands-7m-for-new-protein-program/)

**Simon Kohl（Latent Labs）** — 前 DeepMind AlphaFold2 团队 Simon Kohl 创立的 Latent Labs 于 3 月发布 Latent-Y，一款可从文本提示全自动设计抗体的 AI 代理：九个靶标验证成功六个（67%），亲和力单数纳摩尔级别，速度较专家估算快 56 倍。 / Latent Labs (founded by ex-DeepMind AlphaFold2 lead Simon Kohl) launched Latent-Y, an autonomous antibody design agent operating from text prompts; lab-validated 67% target success rate at single-digit nM affinities, 56× faster than expert estimates.
- 来源: [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/business/latent-y-the-autonomous-ai-agent-for-drug-design-at-scale) | [BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260323538522/en/Latent-Y-The-Autonomous-AI-Agent-for-Drug-Design-at-Scale) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29727)

**Profluent / Ali Madani** — Profluent 与礼来（Eli Lilly）达成最高 22.5 亿美元战略合作协议，用 AI 设计位点特异性重组酶，目标攻克传统基因编辑无法精确触及的难治遗传病。 / Profluent signed a strategic partnership with Eli Lilly worth up to $2.25B to develop AI-designed site-specific recombinases for genetic medicines previously beyond reach of conventional gene editing.
- 来源: [BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260428698315/en/Profluent-Announces-Strategic-Partnership-with-Lilly-to-Develop-AI-Designed-Recombinases-for-Genetic-Medicine)

**Cradle Bio / Stef van Grieken** — AI 蛋白质工程平台 Cradle 与丹麦神经科学药企 Lundbeck 达成合作（6 月 3 日），将 AI 驱动的蛋白设计引入 CNS 抗体开发，是 Lundbeck 首次端到端 AI 指导的蛋白质工程工作流。 / Cradle Bio partnered with Lundbeck (June 3) to deploy its AI protein design platform for CNS antibody development—Lundbeck's first fully AI-guided protein engineering workflow.
